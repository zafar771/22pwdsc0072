Nutrition Guide

1st Day

  • Scrambled eggs (3) with spinach and whole-grain toast (2 slices) 1 banana
  • Greek yogurt (1 cup) with honey and mixed berries
  • Grilled chicken breast (150g) with quinoa (1 cup) and steamed broccoli

2nd Day

  • Oatmeal (1 cup) topped with sliced apple and cinnamon
  • Protein shake with almond milk
  • Stir-fried tofu with mixed vegetables and brown rice (1 cup)

    • 3rd Day

    • Smoothie with spinach, banana, protein powder, and almond butter
    • Cottage cheese (1 cup) with pineapple chunks
    • Beef stir-fry with bell peppers and brown rice (1 cup)

    4th Day

    • Whole grain pancakes (2) topped with berries and a drizzle of maple syrup
    • Quinoa salad with black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, and cilantro
    • Sliced bell peppers with guacamole
    • Baked chicken thighs (150g) with roasted Brussels sprouts and quinoa

    5th Day

    • Chia seed pudding made with almond milk and topped with kiwi and coconut flakes
    • Trail mix with nuts, seeds, and dried fruit
    • Tuna salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and balsamic vinaigrette
    • Grilled steak (150g) with mashed sweet potatoes and green beans
